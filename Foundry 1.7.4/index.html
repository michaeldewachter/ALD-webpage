Problem Situation

  • Communication

    ALD has a need to create a more intimate customer relationship. This can only be achieved by establishing a direct line of communication with their car drivers. This channel will gain customer insights.

  • Product 
    differentiation

    The challenge which ALD is currently facing is creating a differentiation factor that could set them apart from their competitors. This is easier said than done when dealing with a commodity product. This is why product differentiation is depending on the communication between ALD and its drivers.

  • Promotions

    ALD already has products and services in place to create an additional value to the leasing experience of their customers and drivers. But in order to secure a successful integration of these products and services, a stable communication channel with their customers will have to be created.

  • Driving
    behaviour

    ALD has no information regarding their driver's behavior. But because this information is absent, ALD is not able to profile their drivers which could gain a highly added value when ALD wants to increase their product differentiation.

ALL-in-One problem definition 

How can ALD place an app in the hands of all its drivers in order to get to know each of their behavior and needs according to which ALD can offer new leasing products and create long term relationships?

CarCare provides the answer!

This problem can be addressed by implementing CarCare, a simple and easy green cleaning process (accessible via a mobile app) which allows drivers to enjoy on-site cleaning services. By doing this ALD will be provided with valuable data regarding their cars and its drivers collected during each service (3 times per year).

This will be creating a competitive advantage and would be giving the company the possibility of cross selling, setting up new pricing strategies and maximizing the value of their vehicles at the end of the contract. 

the Procedure 

  • 1. Push notification
    First contact with driver

    In one year CarCare will contact the driver three times a year to schedule a cleaning service. This will be possible through the communication platform supported by the application. To gain access to this platform, the driver will first have to sign up. This will make it easy to track and monitor each of the driver's behavior and interests.

    There will also be a possibility to communicate directly to CarCare service-support. This will create and ensure a higher involvement of the client/driver which could then potentially improve customer relationship.

  • 2. Cleaning service
    Cleaning Appointment has been REGISTERED

    When the driver has send all the necessary information regarding the appointment, two CarCare Cleaning Assistants will be appointed to providing that client our service. This service will be performed at the company of that specific driver. By combining all appointments per company, each team of CarCare Cleaning Assistants will be able to service 14 cars per day.

    When CarCare Cleaning Assistants are on-site, they will be able to locate the cars ( scheduled for service that day) in a predefined location. After collecting all the car keys and all the necessary information via the database, the Cleaning Assistants will proceed with the cleaning service. When the car has been cleaned, a thorough assessment can take place in which new data will be collected, the state of de vehicle will be inspected (pinpoint damages by taking pictures) and a driver rating defined. This update will automatically be transferred to the database. Finally a personal promotion (defined by CarCare or ALD) will be placed inside the car with a personal activation code to monitor individual activities.

  • 3. Car assessment
    ASSESSMENT IS NECESSARY TO INSPECT DAMAGES AND CREATE A DRIVER RATING

    When the car has been cleaned, a thorough assessment can take place in which new data will be collected. During this procedure the state of de vehicle will be inspected by pinpointing damages. When a new damages are being spotted, the Cleaning Assistant will report this by taking pictures of the damaged area. All this information will be automatically updated on the database.

    This information will also be used in order to support a "Driver Rating" - system. This system can then be used to incentivizing better driving behavior and which will evidently result in higher reselling price of the vehicle at the end of the contract.

  • 4. Promotions / offerings
    Providing drivers personalized promotions

    Finally a personal ALD promotion or offering will be placed inside the car. Which type of promotion or offering will be defined by previous interests of the driver or can be defined by ALD. CarCare will also provide a direct channel of communication between ALD and their customers regarding this topic via the application on the clients Smartphone

    In order for a client to accept such a promotion or offering, a personal activation code will have to be entered. This will allow us to monitor and track individual activities and interests. This information regarding activated and non-activated promotions will also be visible on the database.

  • 5. ACCESSING DATABASE
    Giving ALD full access to obtained information

    On the database, all the collected information regarding the driver and their activities will be projected. Spreading this information across the time span of the contact (and previous contracts), ALD can draw conclusions from each or their drivers regarding their behavior and activities.

    Via this platform (which will be connected with the CarCare application), ALD will be able to communicate with each driver individually regarding their activities, promotions or offerings. ALD will also be given the opportunity to contact ALD if they have any questions or remarks regarding a specific individual driver.

Driver - CarCare Interface

notifying Drivers 

After signing in, the driver will be able to access the communication platform through the CarCare - application. During this notification procedure, the driver is asked to select one of the, by CarCare, predefined dates. Afterwards the driver is asked when he or she will be needing the car back that day. This will define the schedule during that day and that company visit. After all the information has been filled in, the notification can be send back to CarCare by pressing the SEND button.

Driver - ALD interface

Activate or ask information regarding promotions

This interface will also act as a direct channel of communication via which ALD will be able to contact the driver and notify him of her of upcoming promotions or offerings personally and independently. It is also here that drivers can activate personal (ALD) promotions or offerings left in their car during their previous CarCare-service.

Database interface 

ALD can retrieve all gained information via database

Via the database, all the gained information regarding driver profile, state of car and activated promotions are all projected on each driver's personal page, which ALD will be able to access. It will also be possible to communicate directly to each specific driver though the CarCare-application. This interface will also allow ALD to easily communicate with CarCare and keep us updated regarding modified or added promotions or offerings.

BENEFITS OF THIS TOTAL INTEGRATED SYSTEM

  • Differentiation

    Through implementation of this application ALD will gain new insights regarding their drivers behavior (Driver Rating - System). Based on this information, ALD can provide its clients with contract offers with different pricing structures. ALD can also include information gained by personalized promotions (promote electrical vehicles). 

  • Communication

    By being able to communicate with their clients or customers, ALD can gain specific insights. These insights can be used to create more value towards the client and personalize their offerings. This will ultimately increase customer intimacy and improve customer relationship.

  • CROSS SELLING

    By the use personalized products, promotions and offerings via CarCare, ALD will be able to dramatically increase cross selling of its products.

  • Lock - in

    By increasing customer interaction and customer intimacy, ALD will be able to lock-in their clients. This will be enforced by providing them personalized products and offerings. All these factors will evidently create effective exit-barriers for ALD's clients.

  • Better driving

    By implementing the Driver Rating - system, supported by the assessment of the vehicles each 4 months, the drivers can be incentivized for a better driving behavior. This can once again be reinforced by attaching a bonus points to the Driver Rating -system. The system can even encourage people to drive less with their cars and more with their bicycles.

  • State of car

    By incentivizing drivers to drive better and safer via the Driver Rating - system. Drivers will be ultimately take better care of their vehicles which provides ALD with a higher resell price of the vehicle after the end of the contract.
